I am a Senior Lecturer (Equiv. to Associate Professor in North America) in Cyber Security & Software Systems with School of Computing Technologies at RMIT University. My current research interests include System Privacy and Security, Distributed Systems, Edge and Cloud Computing, and Sustainable Computing. I am a senior member of IEEE and a member of ACM. I also serve as an Associate Editor for IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing (TDSC), IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing (TMC) and IEEE Transactions on Services Computing (TSC), as well as a Review Board Member of the IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems (TPDS).

I am actively looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students with interests in system security and privacy, and distributed ML systems. If you are interested, please send me an email with your CV, transcript and research experience (e.g. minor thesis, publication, etc.) at any time. Visiting scholars and students as well as industry partners are welcomed.
